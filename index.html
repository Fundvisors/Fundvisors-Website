Beyond the six services

Asset verification & tagging

Once a business is running, its books are only as accurate as its assets. We physically verify what you own, tag it for tracking, and reconcile it against your fixed asset register — so your balance sheet matches reality.

  • Physical verification of property, plant, equipment and IT assets against your books.
  • Barcode / QR / RFID tagging of every asset for fast, ongoing tracking.
  • Reconciliation reporting that flags mismatches, missing items and idle assets.
  • Audit-ready documentation your statutory auditor and insurer can both rely on.

Questions founders ask

Frequently asked questions

I'm still at the idea stage — is it too early to talk to you?
No — the earlier the better. Choosing the right entity type before you register saves you from a costly restructure later, and that conversation is exactly where our startup support begins.
My company is already registered. Can I join the path partway through?
Yes. Most of our clients arrive after incorporation, looking for compliance, finance, HR or accounting support specifically. You can start with any single stage and add the others as you need them.
Which entity type should I choose — Pvt Ltd, LLP, OPC or Proprietorship?
It depends on funding plans, liability comfort and how many founders are involved. A Private Limited company suits businesses planning to raise investment; an LLP suits professional partnerships; an OPC suits a single founder wanting limited liability; a Proprietorship suits small, low-risk operations.
How does compliance support actually keep me out of trouble?
We track your statutory filing calendar — annual returns, labour law filings, license renewals — and flag each one ahead of its due date, rather than after you've missed it.
Is the consultancy advice really included at no extra cost?
Yes — when you hire a support service, the expert consultancy that comes with it is part of that service, not a separately billed add-on.
Do you help with fixed asset verification, not just filings?
Yes — alongside the six core services, we run physical asset verification and barcode/QR tagging drives, reconciling what you actually own against your fixed asset register so your books and your audit hold up together.
